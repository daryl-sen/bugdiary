About the Author

Daryl

I'm Daryl - an undergraduate psychology student, an assistant lab manager at SFU Vision lab, and a research assistant at SFU Helping and Happiness lab.

As a hobbyist programmer, I enjoy creating online tools that help others and myself complete daily tasks. This is how BugDiary.com was created! During these times of social isolation, I've built an online platform for all my colleagues and I to collaborate on while working from home.

Despite BugDiary still being in its testing phase, it has gained popularity and support from professors and graduate students who are transitioning into running online experiments (testimonials to come).
